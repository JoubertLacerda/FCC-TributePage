Elon Musk's rise

how it all started:

  • June 28,1971 Elon Musk is born to Father whos an Engineer and a mother whos a Model
  • by 1979 at the age of 8, he was reading books 10 hours a day and had read the Encyclopedia Britannica, TWICE!
  • By age of 12, Elon Musk had learned BASIC by completing a 6-month course in 3 days, and built and sold 'Blastar' video game.
  • 1988 at age 17 he aquires Canadian citizendship and moves to Canada. Lives with relatives and works odd jobs, lands a job shoveling dirt from a boiler room at $18 an hour.
  • 1989 he starts college in Ontario, shortly after that lands an intership at a bank by calling the top Executive, turns out to be a genius at banking but gets scolded by executive for using their coffee machine. starts selling computer out of his dorm room shortly before transfering to UPen where he would eventually earn a double major in Physics and Economics.
  • 1992 runs a speakeasy out of his house in college
  • 1994 gets Wharton economics degree and later physics degree.
  • in 1995 after dropping out of his Phd at Stanford 2 days in, Elon starts coding 'Zip2' with his brother. Gets $28,000 from his father to start with and spends 3 months sleeping in his office.
  • 1996 Elon manages to raise $3M in investment money for 'Zip2', but shortly after that he gets pushed out from the CEO position to CTO by investors.
  • In 1999 Elon Musk makes $22M from 'Zip2' sale, later that year Elon starts his own bank, X.com, with his own money. In that same year, he suffers a car accident in his McLaren F1 that he bought for a cool $1M, All while competing for customers with another startup banking company called 'Confinity'
  • The year 2000 was a busy year for Elon Musk. 'Confinity' and 'X.com' merge to form 'PayPal', Elon marries College girlfriend Justine, Gets dropped as CEO of PayPal while on his honeymoon but still decides to invest on it, almost DIES from maleria he contracted while on vacation, moves to LA to live closer to the Rocket industry and proceeds to learn how to build rockets from books.
  • In 2002 he realised that rockets prices are artificially inflated after failing to negotiate a purchase of a rocket from the Russians. Decides to start a company to build his own rockets, using his own money, and calles it 'SpaceX'. Later that year PayPal is sold and Elon Musk makes a cool $180M and begins to think about investing in Electric car.
  • 2004 he invests on the company called Telsa
  • 2006 was a series of ups and downs for SpaceX. The first ever rocket they built crashes, later that year SpaceX secures contracts to demo cargo delivery to the ISS space station from NASA and make the 'Falcon 9' rocket delivery system. Elon Musk also becomes the 1st investor on a company called 'Solar City'.
  • Between 2007-2008 the 2nd and 3rd rocket launch fails destroying 3 satellites, one of which belonged to NASA. Tesla delivers the first Roadster which has the price tag of $109,000. But both Tesla and SpaceX are low on funds at this stage, luckly the 4th launch from SpaceX is successful which leads to a contract with NASA for 12 flights for wopping $1.2B saving the company from collapse. Speaking of close calls, just hours before Tesla goes bankrupt, he manages to secure funding for the company.
  • 2010 buys car company for cheap from Toyota.
  • In 2011 he promises the world that he would take Humanity to Mars by 2021-2031 and annouces reusable rockets which would bring the cost of space flight down making it 100 times cheaper.
  • The years 2012 and 2014, Elon Musk reviels the Model X, Teslas first SUV range, the 'Dragon' docks with the ISS space station and the Model S starts shipping at $75,000. SpaceX begins trials on landing rockets from low altitude and later begin trials from Orbit onto barges at sea.
  • At this stage its safe to say that Elon Musk in on a roll! In 2015 SpaceX succeeds in landing upright making it the FIRST reusable rocket, 2017 he begins working on the 'BFR' Rocket (Big Fucking Rocket) that works on cheap methane fuel, start the 'Boring'company to make tunnels.
  • 2017 he unveils Tesla first electric truck and the new Roadster capable of accelerating from 0 to 60mph in 1.9 seconds, the Model 3 also begins to ship with a price tag of $35,000
